Our project focuses on improving healthcare data management through the creation of a relational database that aligns with SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being.
Chosen SDG:
SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all ages.
Goal:
The goal is to streamline healthcare operations, enhance patient care, and enable data-driven decision-making through the efficient management and analysis of healthcare data.
